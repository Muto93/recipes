Crispy Fried Chicken Recipe


Can you hear the sizzling?

Fried Chicken is Soul Food!
Prepare to delight your taste buds with our Crispy Fried Chicken recipe. Tender, juicy chicken pieces marinated in a secret blend of spices, dipped in a perfectly seasoned batter, and fried to golden perfection. The result? Irresistibly crispy and flavorful fried chicken that's crunchy on the outside and incredibly juicy on the inside. Whether you're hosting a picnic, game day gathering, or simply craving some fried goodness, this recipe promises a finger-licking experience that will have everyone coming back for seconds. It's comfort food at its finest!

Ingredients:

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour for coating
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Make The Breading

    Combine the flour, paprika, salt, and pepper in a zip-top bag. Seal the bag and shake until well-combined.

  2. Dredge the Chicken

    Pour buttermilk into a shallow bowl, then dip the chicken (on both sides) until it's completely soaked in buttermilk. Place the soaked chicken in the bag with flour mixture, seal, and shake well to coat. Transfer the coated chicken to a baking sheet, and cover. Allow the flour coating to become a paste-like consistency.

  3. Fry the Chicken

    Pour the oil in a skillet and bring it to high heat. Brown the chicken on both sides, then reduce the heat, cover the skillet, and cook for about 30 minutes. Remove the cover, return the heat to high, and fry until extra crispy. Drain the chicken on paper towels.
